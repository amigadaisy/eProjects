FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick Answers to the Most Popular Questions

When you have questions, you can depend on HDD to be ready and able to answer. We're committed to educating our customers and being available to help in any way possible. But first, take a look at a few of the questions other HDD customers often have—you may find your answer right here.

I need help troubleshooting a problem with my equipment.

HDD also provides a network of troubleshooting technicians at HDD distribution centers across the country.

Where can I find literature about my HDD products, such as owner’s manuals, installation guides and product specifications?

Our thorough product documents div has complete information with manuals and guides. Just use the search tool to locate the right information for your model.

Which HDD equipment will best fit my home?

There are many factors that affect the sizing and specifications of your system, including square footage, insulation, window surface and configuration, geographic location of your home, duct sizing and arrangement, and many others. HDD can perform an in-home load analysis to determine which equipment combinations will perfectly suit your home and your family's needs.

What does my warranty cover?

Under the standard factory limited warranty, HDD covers defects in material and workmanship during the warranty period. If a defect is found within the warranty period, HDD will provide a new or remanufactured part, at HDD’s sole option, without charge for the part itself. The limited warranty does not cover labor. A warranty certificate was included with your homeowner information packet, and is specific to the model numbers, serial numbers and installation dates of your products.

How much will a new system cost?

Please contact your HDD for pricing.

What is the life expectancy of my unit?

HDD products are among the longest lasting, most dependable heating and cooling products available. For your specific equipment, there are many variables that affect life expectancy, especially the regularity of proper routine maintenance.

How do I leave a review for my product?

You will receive an email asking you to rate your experience with your product after 6 months. If you didn't receive email to review your product or you haven't registered an older product and would like to review it, visit the product page on this website and leave a review.
